Our Story

SolFinder Research carries out Research, Development, Testing, Maintenance and Benchmarking of latest technologies in computer hardware, software, accessories, electronics, telecommunication, embedded systems, mechatronic systems and energy supplies aiming to provide products and services which can address requirements of customers and end users in India and abroad.

The company also carries out sale, leasing, stocking, trading, import, export, marketing and promotion activities for such products and services.

It imparts training, advisory and consultancy services to interested recipients on the knowledge acquired in pursuit of these activities.
